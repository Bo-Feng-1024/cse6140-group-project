\section{Problem Definition}
% Formal definition of Minimum Vertex Cover.
Given an undirected graph $G = (V, E)$, a \emph{vertex cover} is a subset $C \subseteq V$ such that for every edge $(u, v) \in E$, at least one of $u$ or $v$ belongs to $C$. The \textbf{Minimum Vertex Cover} problem asks to find
\[
C^* = \arg\min_{C \subseteq V} |C| \quad \text{subject to} \quad \forall (u,v) \in E,\ u \in C \text{ or } v \in C.
\]
